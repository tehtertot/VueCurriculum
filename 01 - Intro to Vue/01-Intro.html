Introduction to Vue.js

Objectives:

  • Understand the purpose of front-end frameworks
  • Highlight the pros and cons of Vue
  • Compare Vue to other popular frameworks

Now that you know the fundamentals of the web, you get to learn one of the frameworks that is revolutioning how web applications are being designed and developed. Consider a shopping or social media site where you scroll to the bottom, and new products or stories suddenly appear. With traditional HTML, we'd have to make a new request and render a new page to show the next section of items. (Can you think of a site where you have to click "Next" and the whole page re-renders?) When you learned jQuery, you started to see how interaction can begin to happen.

Vue, React, and Angular each approach the problem differently, but are similar in their overall goal. These front-end frameworks go beyond jQuery by providing a pre-defined structure for managing DOM manipulation.
